G4 Palm Trees

4. PALM TREES

4.1 Whenever PTO Terrain (G.1) is in effect, all orchard hexes become palm tree hexes.15 Each palm tree hex is treated as a normal orchard hex except as stated otherwise. Palm trees are always in season, and their Kindling and Spread numbers are "11".  AFV Riders are allowed in palm tree hexes. [errata included]
