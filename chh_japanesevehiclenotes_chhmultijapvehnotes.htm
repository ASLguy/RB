JAPANESE MULTI-APPLICABLE VEHICLE NOTES

 

A.  The MA and all MG have B11. This is signified by "B11" in red on the counter (bold in the Vehicle Listing).

 

B.  sD becomes available in 1941 or 1944—as signified by the superscript "1+" or "4+" respectively.

 

C.  This tank has no radio in scenarios set prior to 1945 (see D14). As of 1945 it is radio-equipped.
