O4 Single-Hex Two-Story Building

 

4. SINGLE-HEX TWO-STORY BUILDING

 

4.1 A building with a large white center dot (EX: hex 16) is a 1½-level LOS obstacle with both a ground-and first-level Location and an Inherent stairwell. Like a Single Story House (B23.21) it has no Rooftop Location. In all other respects it is a normal building.
