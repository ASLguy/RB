O8 Gully Hexside Entrance

 

8. GULLY ENTRANCE HEXSIDE

 

8.1 A hex containing a small portion of dark green gully artwork (EX: hex DD2) along one of its hexsides (EX: hexside CC3-DD2) is not a gully hex, but that hexside is a gully hexside. When a unit IN a gully hex crosses such a hexside to a non-gully hex (or vice versa), it is not changing elevation.
