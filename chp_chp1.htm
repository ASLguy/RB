P1 Pine Woods

 

1. PINE WOODS

 

1.1 KGP includes a new type of terrain representing extensive stands of pine (evergreen) woods.1 On the KGP maps, the standard "woods-green" background color in a woods hex indicates a pine-woods hex (EX: StKK13) while a normal (B13) woods hex (EX: StLL12) has a thin black outline and no standard background color. A pine woods hex is treated exactly like a normal woods hex except as stated otherwise.

1.2 OBSTACLE HEIGHT: Pine woods are a two-level LOS Obstacle.

1.3 MF COST: Infantry enter pine woods at a cost of 1½ MF (3 MF for Cavalry).
