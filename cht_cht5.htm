5.0 PRINTED RUBBLE

 

5.1  The BRT map depicts printed rubble Locations (EX: hex K13) all of which are treated exactly as if a corresponding rubble counter were in that Location. Ignore small portions of the artwork extending across a hexside into an adjacent non-rubble Location.

5.2  CLEARANCE: Printed rubble is not considered Fallen and so cannot be cleared (B24.71).
