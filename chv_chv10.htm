V10 City Squares

 

10.  CITY SQUARES10

 

10.1 DESCRIPTION: Any hex containing Paved Road artwork and an enlarged black center-dot (EX: N5, O5, O6, P4, P5) is a City Square hex. All rules for Wide City Boulevards (B7) apply unchanged in these hexes.

10.2 MISCELLANEOUS: Street Fighting (A11.8) and Dash (A4.63) are NA in a City Square hex. VotG5 applies as if the City Square were a paved road.{J8}
