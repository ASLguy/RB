VOTG SPECIFIC VEHICLE NOTES

 

88.1 SdKfz 10/5:  The later versions of the SdKfz 10/4 were equipped with the newer 2.0cm FlaK 38 AA gun; these upgunned versions of the SdKfz 10/4 were given the designation SdKfz 10/5.  For added protection, 8mm armor plate was added to the front of the vehicle cab and gunshield beginning in late 1942.  In 1943 the number of crewmen was reduced from six to four.  Photographic evidence and tactical use suggests that leitche FlaK units participating in the street fighting in Stalingrad were likely equipped with SdKfz 10/5.

†  These are partially Armored AFV, with armor only on their front Target Facing.
