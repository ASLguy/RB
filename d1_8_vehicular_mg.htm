D1.8 VEHICULAR MG

Listed on counter, in vehicle listing as:

BMG/CMG/AAMG

BMG = Bow MG FP/CMG = Coaxial MG FP/AAMG = Anti-Aircraft MG FP; if no AAMG present listing is BMG/CMG. Superscript R# (EX: "R2") after BMG or CMG FP denotes Rear MG (RMG). RMG may only fire through rear VCA (for rear BMG) or rear TCA (for rear CMG). or in own hex. BMG printed over white dot is a Fixed-Mount (D1.81). cannot be Scrounged and receives +1 DRM when firing at a moving/Motion target.
