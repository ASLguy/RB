LINE OF SIGHT (E1.1 - .15)

•Within NVR (NVR halved [FRD] from BU AFV)

•Illuminated Locations

•Moving vehicles (1.5x NVR [FRU], or 2x if tracked)

•Gunflashes (treat as a concealed unit if beyond NVR)

LOS from Illuminated Location to:

•Illuminated Locations

•Gunflashes (treat as a concealed unit)
