NVR CHANGE (E1.12)

•No change First Player Turn

•One hex unless Scattered Clouds

•Minimum NVR is 0, maximum NVR is 6 [EXC: 2 and 9 for Ground/Deep Snow]

•Wind Change DR:

 crd = 6 & wdr:

         ≤ 3: Lowers NVR

         = 4: Increase NVR [EXC: no change after Starshell/IR Placed]

         ≥ 5: Increase NVR
