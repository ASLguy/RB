E1.53; E1.55 Straying

STRAYING (E1.53; 1.55)

Straying NA if:

•LOS to known enemy unit, or Beach/Ocean

•On or ADJACENT to road, gully, stream, path, river bank, or Illuminated Location

•Moving within same hex; entering sewer/tunnel

•IN cave/cave-complex

Movement DR:

         cdr = 6 & wdr = 5-6: Stealthy Strays

                         = 3-6: Normal Strays

                         = 1-6: Lax Strays

Subsequent Straying DR (cdr) determines direction (Jitter Fire on doubles after first attack)

•Move "as far as possible"

•TI if:

•Cannot move one hex without Double Time, Minimum Move, or ESB

•Next Location would require: climbing, using stairwell, fording, scaling, or swimming; moving closer to a Known enemy unit, or offboard; entering an Illuminated Location

•Gains LOS to Beach/Ocean hex
