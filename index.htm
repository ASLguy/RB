What's New?

- New Version numbering scheme, where the version is based on build date rather than anything else.  Version 2.yy.mm.dd.

- many, many graphics have been replaced with their VASL 5 equivalents.  This has made the eRB a LOT smaller, and better looking, I think.  Chapter H has NOT been done this way, since I felt it was important to keep the "real world" counter pics intact.

- ALL the official errata up to the current date is in.  This is usually shown with a highlight either on or after the text.  Sometimes, errata means taking things out, and that looks like this.
