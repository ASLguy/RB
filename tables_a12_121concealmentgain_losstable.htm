A

If it: becomes broken/Reduced/Wounded/ berserk/overstacked/captured,

 

uses Non-Assault Movement,

 

attacks in CC [EXC: an Ambush that eliminates/captures all defenders; A11.4],

 

is in Location that enemy Infantry/Cavalry attempt to enter during the MPh/RtPh,

 

is in an obstacle where a vehicle ends its MPh in Bypass (A15.42),

 

is attacked resulting in at least a PTC (or its corresponding DR, on the * Vehicle line or for OBA [or for an A-T Mine Immobilization result] vs a vehicle) on the IFT [EXC: A-P Mine Attacks; B28.411],

 

is a vehicle hit by ordnance,

 

is in a Location successfully Searched by the enemy,

 

is a Dummy in a building successfully Mopped Up by the enemy,

 

fails a PAATC caused by a vehicle's entry of its Location (A12.41),

 

is attacked by a Sniper dr (A14.3) or WP NMC (A24.31).

 

B

If it/its PRC/its manning Infantry:

 

moves/advances/withdraws into an Open Ground hex (A10.531),

 

is in Motion, expends any vehicular MP/MF,

 

fires or directs fire,

 

fires (not just attempts to use) a Smoke Discharger (D13.3).

 

C

If it engages in any other action e.g.,

 

attempts to entrench, kindle a Fire,

 

Deploy, Recombine,

 

make a Sniper/PF/MOL/ATMM Check,

 

rally a broken unit, use Clearance,

 

apply Leader DRM [EXC: PAATC; Armor Leader],

 

place SMOKE grenades, etc.

 

[EXC to above: using a radio/field phone; Spotting; taking a PAATC (and Leader DRM applied to such a PAATC) not caused by a vehicle entering its Location; SW/Gun repair attempts, dropping SW; taking a Set DC NTC];

 

D

If it/its PRC/its manning Infantry is designated as an Opportunity Firer (A7.25),

 

or changes CA;

 

or if its manning Infantry fire their inherent FP/any SW.

 

E

If it: becomes broken/Reduced/berserk/Wounded/overstacked,

 

is attacked resulting in at least a PTC on the IFT [EXC: A-P Mine attack; B28.411],

 

or is attacked by a Sniper dr (A14.3) or WP NMC(A24.31)

 

F

If it: becomes broken/Reduced/Wounded,

 

is in a Location successfully Searched by the enemy,

 

is a Dummy in a building successfully Mopped Up by the enemy.

 

G

If it: uses IFE; or fires its Gun at a range of zero,

 

or at a range of 1-16 hexes with a colored dr >= 5 in its Original TH DR,

 

or at >= 17 hexes with such a colored dr of 6

 

(the range is that from the nearest Good Order enemy ground unit to the Gun—not necessarily that from the target to the Gun).

 

[EXC: a RCL would automatically lose its "?"; C12.12]

 

H

Its "?" (or HIP for a Fortification) is lost automatically

 

L

If a unit therein loses its "?"/HIP, or if use of its protective DRM is claimed.
